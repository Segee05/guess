D-GUESS
GAME

Kindly Read the Instructions

  1. You will see a word displayed on the screen. This word is randomly chosen from a list of names.
  2. Your task is to click on the buttons provided below the word to match the displayed word.
  3. Each button corresponds to a name from the list.
  4. If you click the correct button that matches the displayed word, you win the game!
  5. If you win, an alert will appear congratulating you, and the game will reset after 2 seconds.
  6. However, if you click the wrong button, you will lose points.
  7. You start with a total of 3 points.
  8. The total points will be displayed on the screen.
  9. Each time you click the wrong button, you will lose 1 point.
  10. If your points reach 0, you will lose the game.
  11. If you lose, an alert will appear, notifying you to try again, and the game will reset after 2 seconds.
  12. The buttons will be shuffled randomly each time you play, so be attentive!
  13. Enjoy the game and have fun!
